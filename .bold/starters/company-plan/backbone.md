1. **Every company plan names a single primary goal for its horizon** — a plan with three co-equal top priorities hasn't actually chosen one, and a business that hasn't chosen doesn't get a plan that pretends otherwise.
   <!-- source: starter(company-plan) -->
   **Status**: enforced

2. **Every roadmap commitment carries an owner and a review date** — an item with neither is a wish, not a commitment, and doesn't belong in the roadmap section.
   <!-- source: starter(company-plan) -->
   **Status**: enforced

3. **Revenue lines are named and separated wherever the plan discusses money** — a plan that talks about "the business" as one undifferentiated revenue stream can't be turned into a chart of accounts or a job-costing model later. This is the seam the `chart-of-accounts` starter picks up.
   <!-- source: starter(company-plan) -->
   **Status**: enforced
